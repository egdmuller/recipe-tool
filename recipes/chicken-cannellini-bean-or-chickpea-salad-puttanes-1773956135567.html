Chicken, Cannellini Bean or Chickpea Salad Puttanesca

A bright and tangy Mediterranean salad featuring protein (chicken, cannellini beans, or chickpeas) tossed with sundried tomatoes, olives, capers, and fresh parsley, finished with a drizzle of optional chile oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups cooked chicken (pulled or cut into bite-size pieces) or cannellini beans or chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 (6 to 8 ounce) jar chopped sundried tomatoes with Italian herbs in oil
  • 1/2 medium red or yellow onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced or pressed
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1 tablespoon caper brine
  • 1/2 cup green olives, halved
  • 1/2 cup black olives, halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive brine
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise (optional)
  • Fine salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Calabrian chile oil, for drizzling (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the chicken or chickpeas, sundried tomatoes (including their oil), onion, garlic, capers, caper brine, olives, olive brine, lemon juice and mayonnaise if using. Stir well. Taste, and season with salt and pepper as desired.
  2. At this point, the salad can be covered and refrigerated for up to 4 days. Before serving, stir in the parsley. Drizzle chile oil on top of each serving, if desired.
